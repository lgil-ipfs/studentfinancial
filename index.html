Who We Are

Money stress is one of the biggest barriers to student success — and we're here to remove it.

StudentFinancial.ca is a free, not-for-profit service dedicated to making financial literacy accessible and judgment-free for every Canadian student. We offer one-on-one counselling with registered advisors, plain-English guides, and tools built specifically for the Canadian system. No fees, no sales pressure, no catch.

Our mission

To give every Canadian student the financial knowledge and support they need to thrive — regardless of their background or income.
