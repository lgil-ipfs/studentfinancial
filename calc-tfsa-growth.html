The TFSA Edge

Unlike a standard savings account, anything you earn inside a Tax-Free Savings Account (TFSA) is 100% yours to keep. You don't pay tax on the interest, dividends, or capital gains. Over 10-20 years, this tax savings can amount to tens of thousands of dollars.
