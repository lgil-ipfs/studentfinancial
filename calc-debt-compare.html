Avalanche vs. Snowball

Compare repayment strategies to see which fits your psychology and your wallet.

Scenario: You have a $5,000 Credit Card balance (19.9%) and $15,000 Student Loan (6%).

Avalanche Savings: $1,420.00
Snowball Speed: 1 month faster win

Which one should you choose?

The Debt Avalanche: You pay off the debt with the highest interest rate first. This is mathematically superior and saves you the most in interest.

The Debt Snowball: You pay off the debt with the smallest balance first. This provides a "psychological win" earlier, which can help stay motivated if you have many small debts.
